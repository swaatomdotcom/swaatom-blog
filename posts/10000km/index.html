10,000 kilometres

• 1 min read

Cycling has to be one of the best things that anybody can do. Transport, exercise and stress relief all combined in one easy package. I took a short break on my commute into Phnom Penh to capture this moment. Me and my trusty Giant bicycle were about to mark a metric milestone together.

My bicycle: Giant Rincon Disc

Check out the odometer reading: 9,999 kilometres. That’s a lot of riding. Time well spent, money well spent (and saved).

Odometer: 9,999 kilometers

I haven’t really done much riding since - I got a new job closer to home, so my commute is only about five minutes now. When all this covid nonsense is over though, I think I’ll ride it out on a serious tour. Something to look forward to.
