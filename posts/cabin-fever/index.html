Cabin fever

• 2 min read

Here I am, stuck inside four walls in my room in Ta Khmau. There’s no official lockdown here, but it makes sense to limit social contact. I’m gonna escape for a while tomorrow though, and head across the river to visit the family. I think that I’m pretty well socially distanced riding a bicycle. I’m looking forward to it. I’m in two minds whether to take the ferry though (very crowded), or ride all the way up to the Prek Tamak bridge and back south again. That will add about 30km on to my journey, I reckon about 50km in total. Not that far, but I haven’t had a decent ride in ages. See how I feel in the morning. Either way, I need to start out early - as soon as there’s enough light to ride.

It will be nice to see everyone again. It’s been a bit lonely here, but the isolation is necessary given the circumstances. I have another week before the new term starts, and I guess this might be the last opportunity I have to visit for a while. Hopefully I will get some nice pictures to post here. I have a feeling that finding content for the new blog is going to be a good thing for me.
